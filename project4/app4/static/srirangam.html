The Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple in Srirangam is one of the most magnificent temples in India — rich in architecture, spirituality, and history.



The main deity is Lord Ranganatha, a reclining form of Lord Vishnu on the serpent Adisesha.
The temple is considered the first and foremost of the 108 Divya Desams (sacred Vishnu temples praised in Tamil Vaishnavite hymns).

One of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world.
Covers 156 acres with 7 concentric enclosures (prakarams) and 21 gopurams (tower gateways).
The Rajagopuram (main tower) is 72 meters tall – one of the tallest temple towers in Asia.
