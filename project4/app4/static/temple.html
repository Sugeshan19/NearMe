The Uchi Pillayar Temple is another iconic and spiritually significant site in Tiruchirappalli (Trichy), Tamil Nadu.
Perched high on a rock, it’s both a sacred site and a historical landmark with stunning views of the city and rivers.



Location:
Atop the Rockfort Hill, in the heart of Trichy city
About 83 meters (273 feet) above ground level

Rock-Cut Architecture:
The temple is carved out of a massive ancient rock that’s over 3.8 billion years old – older than the Himalayas!

Climb to Divinity:
Around 400 steps take you to the shrine, offering panoramic views of:

Cauvery and Kolidam Rivers
Srirangam Island
Trichy city skyline

Photography Spot:
It’s a favorite for sunrise/sunset views and cityscapes.
