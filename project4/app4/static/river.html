The Kolidam River (also spelled Coleroon) is a major distributary of the
Cauvery River (Kaveri) in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

Origin:
The Kolidam River originates near the town of Grand Anicut (Kallanai Dam) in Tiruchirapalli district,
where the Cauvery River splits into two:

Southern branch continues as Cauvery
Northern branch becomes the Kolidam

Length:
Approximately 150 kilometers long.
