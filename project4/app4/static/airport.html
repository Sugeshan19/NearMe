Tiruchirappalli International Airport (IATA: TRZ), commonly known as Trichy Airport,
is located approximately 5 kilometers south of Tiruchirappalli city in Tamil Nadu, India.
It is the third busiest airport in the state, following Chennai and Coimbatore,
ranks 31st in India for total passenger traffic as of 2022.



Key Features

Terminals: The airport spans over 702 acres and comprises two terminals.
Terminal 1 handles both domestic and international flights,
while Terminal 2 is dedicated to cargo operations.


Airlines and Destinations:
Airport serves as a hub for several airlines, including
Air India Express
IndiGo
Malindo Air Scoot
SriLankan Airlines.
It connects to various international destinations such as Abu Dhabi, Colombo, Dubai, Kuala Lumpur, and Singapore.
